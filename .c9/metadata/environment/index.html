Our History

\n

\n Launched in 2006 by childhood friends Sally Blundle and Rosie Davenport, Wild Bunch is at the heart of London's flower scene. Originally based in Chapel Market, Islington, Sally and Rosie moved to their current Soho home in 2009.\n

\n
\n
\n \n

Our Services

\n

\n We are ready and able to supply the highest quality flower arrangements 365 days a year. From the smallest 'love you' to the grandest event, we give 100% personal attention to every special moment in your life.\n

\n
\n
\n \n

Our Eco Credentials

\n

\n All our amazing flowers are sourced from our organic suppliers based just outside Brighton in the beautiful South Downs, Sussex. Our all-electric deliverly vehicles help make London's streets a healthier place to breathe.\n
